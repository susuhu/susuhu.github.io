Leveraging Deep Neural Fields for Navigation of Middle Ear Diagnostics

2022-2023 German Cancer Research Center - NCT Dresden

Current diagnostics for middle ear infection with Otoscopy only cover single aspects of the pathology. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) provides a comprehensive investigation of both the morphology and the function of the middle ear. However, the interpretation of OCT volumetric data remains difficult up to now. Hence, a substantial improvement is anticipated in merging OCT images and volumes with a priori knowledge.

Mixed Intra Layer CNN Quantization for CIM Architectures

2022 Fraunhofer IPMS

Deep Convolutional neural networks (CNNs) have proven SOTA performance in computer vision tasks, but they require high computation resources, which makes it harder to deploy on hardware. Quantization is a common way to reduce the demand on hardware capability. This project presents that layer-by-layer mixed precision quantizaton for both weights and activations with respect to their sensitivity, enables signification reduction in the number of MAC operations and memory footprint, while maintaining a close level of accuracy compared to full precision level.
